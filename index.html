We are Islandwide Famous!

Fann Wong and Christopher Lee attended
our Baking Workshop!

Fann Wong and Christopher Lee brought Zedd to our Parent & Child Baking Workshop held in September this year! The Baking Workshop aims to bring learning outside of the classroom by allowing parents to bond with their kid through baking of pastries and tarts together. Zedd has been a Smart Tuition student since he started preschool and has recently signed up for the upcoming K2 - P1 bridging programme. It’s never too early to be prepared for an important transition to Primary School!

Vivian Lai celebrate her two daughters
academic success!

It was a prideful moment for our local celebrity, Vivian Lai, as she heard about the grades her two daughters, Ariel and Vera, achieved in PSLE and O Levels respectively in the same year. Both Ariel and Vera joined our Smart Tuition family three years ago, and has been working hard ever since to prepare for the national exams. We are equally happy and proud of their hard-earned achievements!

Kate Pang and Andie Chen joined us for our Singapore Zoo Bonding Session!

A total of 10 families registered for our special bonding trip to the Singapore Zoo, alongside that we have Kate Pang and Andie Chen! The local celebrities did not visit the Singapore Zoo before due to tight schedules and were greatly appreciative of the family bonding session they had with their children, Aden and Avery. In Smart Tuition, we believe relationships with parents play a crucial role in a kid’s achievement and mentality, so be sure to keep a lookout on our future outdoor bonding sessions!
